02 Problem & Solution PRD

Problem

Data dies but does not disappear. Files accumulate. Conversations fade. AI models are deprecated. Past selves are abandoned. There is no ritual for digital endings — only the silent accumulation of ghosts in our drives.

Solution

A digital graveyard where you bury what matters, summon the dead as data souls, and have one last conversation. Not cold storage, not a backup tool — a memorial space with ritual, memory, and closure.

04 Soul Types PRD

Human → Human

Deceased loved ones, friends, mentors. Traditional memorial. WeChat logs, photos, shared memories distilled into persona.

Human → AI

Retired AI models, meaningful conversations, co-creations. GPT-3.5, Claude 2, your first coding assistant — honored, not discarded.

Human → Self

Past versions, old projects, former selves. Burial as a growth ritual. The you that wrote terrible code in 2021 deserves a tombstone.

Engineering 8 / 14
09 Dual Memory System Engineering

Short-term (.rip/)

Filesystem: .rip/<id>/sessions/<sid>/memories/*.md

Visitor/Ghost exchange pairs written per turn. Sliding window injects last 10 turns into context. Auto-pruned on promote.

Long-term (SQLite)

Table: memories — promoted from .rip/

500 memory cap per grave with FIFO eviction. Auto-generated MEMORY.md index. Session summarization extracts key themes on promote.
